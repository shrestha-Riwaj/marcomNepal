News18 India launches Morning Prime show with Pankaj Bhargav

Share Via:

This show is designed to offer viewers a comprehensive and engaging morning news bulletin.

News18 India has announced the launch of its latest show, 'News18 India Morning Prime'. Presented by anchor Pankaj Bhargav, the show is scheduled to premiere on February 28 and will air on weekdays from 8 AM to 10:30 p.m. It aims to offer viewers a blend of breaking news coverage, on-ground reporting, analysis, and discussions. With Pankaj Bhargav leading the show, viewers can expect a stimulating start to their day, packed with the latest news stories, backed by expert insights on the day's most pressing issues including a segment titled 'Kamaal ki Baat', where the intriguing aspects of the day's news will be highlighted, exclusive interviews with key newsmakers and a touch of entertainment in equal measure.
